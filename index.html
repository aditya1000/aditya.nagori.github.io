About

Embarking on a journey where the realms of science and the essence of human curiosity converge, I traverse through the path of biomedical data science and computational medicine. Very early amidst the whispers of knowledge and the spirit of inquiry, my quest for understanding the secrets of life through data began.

The pursuit of wisdom led me to the hallowed halls of the CSIR-IGIB, New Delhi, AIIMS, New Delhi and IIIT Delhi where I delved deep into the arcane arts of AI and computational medicine, crafting a Ph.D. thesis that sought to predict the critical events in the critically ill and asthmatics children. My work weaves together the threads of machine learning, and statistical models to foresee and forestall the disease.

Currently, I am a Data Scientist and Post-doctoral Fellow at the illustrious UT Southwestern Medical Center. Here, I am delved into cardiovascular research, employing the alchemy of data to gauge the health of hearts at risk, and to spread the light of digital health literacy across the land.

Yet, beyond the veil of numbers lies a soul attuned to the elemental rhythms of existence. In the harmonies of music, I find the universal language that binds all creation. Through the dance of the shuttlecock and the embrace of the water, I celebrate the physical form in its motion and tranquility, a testament to the balance between the cerebral and the corporeal.

In this nexus of science, I extend an invitation to fellow travelers and seekers of truth. Together, let us explore the mysteries of existence, weaving data into the fabric of life, transforming the unknown into the understood. Let us embark on this journey, where each data point is a star in the vast cosmos of knowledge, guiding us toward enlightenment and the betterment of humanity.
